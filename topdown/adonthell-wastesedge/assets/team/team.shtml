Alexandre Courbot (gnurou)
  • Background: Studies computer science at Lille university, France.
  • Personal note: Started the Genescroll project before joining Kai's Adonthell project as programmer. Working on the map engine.
  • Vision of the future:
    • More developers join Adonthell, making it the best RPG ever
    • During the same time, Free Software becomes more and more present in the computing and games industry
    • Finally, everybody can have software for free that runs on cheap machines
    • Penguins take over the world
  • Links: diary, website
Andrew Phillips
  • Background: Computer Science Major, Hardin-Simmons University, Abilene, Texas, USA.
  • Personal note: I found Adonthell while looking for open-source/free software role-playing games. I stuck with it because of those that I found, few impressed me. Adonthell is on the short list of worthwhile free software CRPGs.
  • Vision of the future:
    • I fill Adonthell with good writing and good stories
    • Adonthell succeeds in all respects: art, engine, story
    • CRPGers have the chance to play a good game without having to think about whether buying games will support the loss of their rights
    • Adonthell has competition, forcing us to get better and giving gamers even more options
  • Links: diary, website
Benjamin Walther-Franks
  • Background: Studies Digital Media at the University and Art School in Bremen, Germany
  • Personal note: Got to know Adonthell through James and have been fascinated by it ever since. Did (nearly) all the character animation for version 0.3, and will probably be stuck with the job for the future ...
    Doing some other graphics too, including the website layout.
  • Vision of the future:
    • More people do graphix for Adonthell --> less work for me
    • I actually get to do some programming for Adonthell, once I learn how to. --> probably more work for the others ...
    • A lot more people join Adonthell, and we actually get stuff done --> :)
    • We finish the complete Adonthell game, and it´s the best game ever made --> possibility rating: 100% and rising
    • Peace and freedom reign the world, and they lived happily ever after --> uh ... I wake up from dreaming.
  • Links: diary
James Nash (cirrus)
  • Background: Main Graphics geezer on the Adonthell project, studies computer science at Warwick university, UK.
  • Personal note: Got involved with Alex's Genescroll doing graphics, came to Adonthell through the Genescroll/Adonthell merge, never looked back :)
  • Vision of the future:
    • The world of Adonthell inspires new quests, books, pictures, comics, fanclubs ...
    • Adonthell gets a multiplayer version, there is an online world inhabited by clever NPCs and real players - a huge new community is born!
    • Millions of windope users migrate to Linux (or other *nixes) to play Adonthell, M$ goes broke!
    • The Adonthell Team gets world famous and rich beyond their wildest dreams thanks to sponsors, donations and merchandise (the game stays free!)
  • Links: diary, website

Joseph Toscano (JT)
  • Background: Freelance videogame music composer.
  • Personal note: I was browsing Freshmeat for an RPG to write music for, and the very first item on the game list was Adonthell. The rest, of course is history.
  • Vision of the future:
    • Adonthell will hopefully be hailed for having rather good music. If so, then I'll know my job was well done. >D
    • Adonthell will become an immensly popular tribute to Linux and how high-quality games can be made on platforms other than WINDOW~1.
  • Links: diary, website
Josh Glover
  • Background: Studies Computer Science at The College of William and Mary in Virginia, USA.
  • Personal note: Got involved with Adonthell when searching for an RPG engine to base my own RPG project on. Decided to hang that project up for a year or so to work on Adonthell.

    • Adonthell is made into a great RPG with the help of gamers, coders, graphic designers, and musicians across the globe.
    • An Adonthell Japanese translation is begun and kept up with the main plotline.
    • I learn more from my fellow team members.
    • <STANDARD CliCHE> World domination! </STANDARD CliCHE>

Kai Sterker (kaiman)
  • Background: Studies computer science at Würzburg University, Germany
  • Personal note: Founded the Adonthell project because back then there were no good commercial RPG's available. Today there are plenty, but I'm too involved with Adonthell to find much time to play them ;).
  • Vision of the future:
    • Adonthell has become the new standard for computer role playing games and is absolutly free
    • After publication of the tools used for creating Adonthell, more astonishing, free role playing games emerge
    • Thus encouraged, similar projects for other software are established
    • The need for commercial software no longer exists, since everything is available for free
  • Links: diary
