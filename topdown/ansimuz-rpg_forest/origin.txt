A complete Pixel Art Assets pack to build  a Top-Down RPG/Adventure  game out of the box.

Features:

    A Complete tileset at 16x16 pixels each tile to build your levels easily
    A lot of different Objects to decorate your levels
    Archer Player character  fully animated in 4 directions
    2 different animated enemies: Treant and Mole
    Waterfall animated, death animation, coins and gems
    Playable Phaser Demo Code Included
    Music by Pascal Belisle
