A 32x32 pixel muted tileset. This is a remix of some old (game) art ideas, reworked and extended with some new stuff.

I would not consider this tileset to be fully polished or even complete, perhaps rather as a base theme for you to build upon and extend.
I am open to updating this though, so if you have any suggestions, request or want to contribute please let me know.
